Where we came from!

Founded in 2021, our cafe prides itself on its rich and flavorful coffees. Our proud founder, Jada Domingue and their family grew up growing coffee beans as a hobby, brewing cups of delicious coffee for their neighbors. The community all agrees that opening this business was the right move, now the world can enjoy their coffee!   

About the Founder

Our founder Jada Domingue is a lover of all things coffee, from growing their own beans from a young age to learning proper ratios of brews, they've taken extra care in ensuring every cup of coffee tastes as perfect as it looks!
